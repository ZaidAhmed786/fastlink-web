Below is a list of the types of Personal Information that we may collect as you interact with our Services:

  • Customer Contact Information. Your Customer Contact Information is any information needed to contact you by any common means, like call, email, or sms, fulfill your orders, and requests, such as your name, address, phone number, fax number, and email address.
  • Demographic Information. Demographic Information is publicly available information that we may learn about you regarding such things as your age, income, home ownership, household size, etc., that helps us to provide you with better offers for products and services.
  • Website Usage Information. Website Usage Information is information that we collect, via anonymous third-party cookies, that tells us about the way you use and navigate through our Websites. For example, we advertise with third parties (for example, business partners, advertising networks) and may receive anonymous information about you from them as you click to come over to our Site. We may combine this anonymous information with other information you have provided to us or which we collect about you.
  • Proprietary Analytics. This Site uses proprietary analytics systems to help improve usability and the customer experience. For deep analysis to provide better services to our customers, we may record mouse clicks, mouse movements, scrolling activity, user agent, Internet protocol (IP) address used to connect your computer to the Internet, and such other information. 

Your Personal Information does not include data where the identity has been removed (anonymous data). We collect, use, and disclose Personal Information to operate our business and as required by law. Please take a moment to read this Notice to learn more about our information practices.

Cookies

Sites operated by fastspeedlinks.com use cookies. A cookie is a piece of data (or other similar technology like a web beacon, locally stored object, or pixel tag) that records your preferences and data about your visit to the Sites. Cookies are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of a site. 

Most internet browsers allow you to erase cookies, block acceptance of cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored. If you block cookies, some portions of the Sites may not function properly, and some features of the Sites may be slow or less efficient. The Sites also allow you to specify your cookie preferences on your first visit, providing a means to protect your information while enabling the Sites to still function.

We use cookies to enable you to navigate between pages and to generally improve your user experience. We also use cookies to analyze how users interact with the Sites, to enable you to share content using social media, and to provide you with embedded content from third parties such as maps or videos. 

We also sometimes use cookie-type technologies in emails to identify how users come to the Sites so that we can evaluate and measure the effectiveness of our email marketing.

We may combine information collected via cookies with your Personal Information to derive statistical information about visitors to the Sites. Where the Sites use third-party cookies, we encourage you to check with the relevant third party about their own separate cookie policies.
